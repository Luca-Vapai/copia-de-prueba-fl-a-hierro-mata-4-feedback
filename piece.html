How to leave feedback
  1. Enter your name in the field on the right (required to submit).
  2. Watch the video. When something needs feedback, pause it.
  3. Click Add comment at current time to anchor a comment to that exact moment, or use Mark range start / Mark range end to anchor it to a range.
  4. Choose the element (Music, Video, etc.), the action (Substitute, Improve, Modify), the priority, and write a clear description.
  5. Use General comment for feedback that does not refer to a specific moment.
  6. You can edit or delete any comment before sending.
  7. When you are done, click Submit feedback at the bottom of the right panel.
